Innovative E-mail Client

Innovative E-mail Client is an e-mail client that brings a different perspective to the e-mail experience. As it allows you to have features of a regular e-mail client, it also allows for a timeline experience which helps you get better insights on your conversations with other people

Innovative E-mail Client is a stateless app consisting of a React.js frontend and an Express.js backend. It uses Google's Gmail API to give you the e-mail experience

Features

You can open the images in new tab to get a closer look

Innovative timeline feature

Innovative timeline feature allows you to track your threads and your thread statistics.

Delete messages

You can delete your messages

E-mail composing

You can compose new e mails and add an html formatted body with the latest rich text editor

E-mail viewing

You can view the e-mails you have received and our application also supports e-mails with an HTML body

Labeled E-mail Folders

You can access the information about your e-mail folders

Star e-mails

You can star your e-mails so you can access them faster

Google Login

You can swiftly login to our application using the Gmail Consent Screen

E-mail listing

You can list your e-mails and browse them as you wish

Mark As Read

Our application will automatically label an e-mail as read when you click on them

Thread History

You can view your thread history and read e-mails from the thread as you wish

Replying To E-mails From A Thread

In our application, you can reply to specific e-mails from a thread you're viewing
